About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research explores practical, hands‑on ways to leverage large language models and other generative tools to streamline research workflows, boost productivity, and spark creativity. Across weekly modules we cover prompt engineering, data analysis with AI copilots, literature review automation, reproducible workflows, ethical use of AI, and crafting compelling AI‑generated presentations and visualizations.
